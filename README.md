## 📊 Insurance Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

### Insurance Analytics Dashboard: Customer Insights, Charges Analysis & Risk Segmentation

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze insurance data and uncover insights related to customer demographics, medical attributes, and factors influencing insurance charges.

### 📌 Description / Purpose

This project focuses on analyzing insurance data to understand how various factors such as region, smoking habits, BMI, age, and number of children impact insurance charges.

The dashboard enables users to:

- Compare insurance charges across regions
- Analyze the impact of smoking on insurance costs
- Understand how BMI and age influence charges
- Explore demographic distributions (gender, children)

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making in pricing strategies and risk assessment.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using:

- Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and visualization
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures and aggregations
- Data Modeling – Structuring relationships and enabling filtering

### 📂 Data Source
- Insurance dataset containing:
- Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Region)
- Health metrics (BMI, Smoking Status)
- Policy attributes (Number of Children)
- Insurance charges

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query before analysis.

### 🚀 Features & Highlights

Insurance companies need to understand:

- What factors drive higher insurance charges
- How customer lifestyle impacts risk
- Regional variations in pricing
- Demographic trends in policyholders

This dashboard provides a consolidated view to analyze these aspects.

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
- Identify key drivers of insurance charges
- Analyze risk factors such as smoking and BMI
- Compare regional trends in pricing
- Enable better customer segmentation and pricing strategy

## 📊 Key Dashboard Insights & Visuals
### 1️. Regional Analysis

Visual: Average BMI & Charges by Region

- Compares average BMI and insurance charges across regions
- Helps identify geographic differences in risk and pricing

Insight:

- Certain regions show higher average charges despite similar BMI levels, indicating additional influencing factors

### 2. Smoking Impact Analysis

Visual: Average Charges by Smoker vs Non-Smoker (Donut Chart)

- Highlights cost differences based on smoking habits

Insight:

- Smokers incur significantly higher insurance charges, making smoking a major risk factor

### 3️. Demographic Distribution

Visual: Male vs Female Count

- Shows gender distribution of policyholders

Insight:

- Nearly balanced distribution with slight variation

### 4️. Charges by Number of Children

Visual: Average Charges by Children (Line Chart)

- Analyzes how number of dependents affects insurance cost

Insight:

- Charges vary with number of children, indicating family size influences premium calculations

### 5️. Age-Based Charges Analysis

Visual: Max & Min Charges by Age Group (Table)

- Shows charge variation across age bins

Insight:

- Insurance charges increase significantly with age, indicating higher risk in older age groups
